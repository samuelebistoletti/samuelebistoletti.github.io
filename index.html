Profile

As Head of IT Infrastructure & Cybersecurity at EOLO S.p.A., Samuele Bistoletti leads the function that safeguards the continuity, security and efficiency of the company's IT infrastructure. Business resilience is the principle guiding strategy and daily operations. The unit operates as an integrated function across three domains (infrastructure, cybersecurity and digital workplace), with a mandate that covers the design and operations of the IT infrastructure, the protection of digital assets, data and critical services, the availability of platform engineering services, and the delivery of a consistent and reliable user experience across the organisation.

With more than thirteen years of experience across software engineering, infrastructure engineering and cybersecurity, he combines technical depth with strategic oversight, aligning security, scalability and business objectives within a complex and highly regulated industry. Throughout his career he has led multi-year transformation programs, established structured security governance frameworks, and strengthened enterprise resilience through prevention, monitoring, standardization and continuous improvement, with a pragmatic approach to risk management and to the economic sustainability of the technology choices he makes.

His leadership is grounded in accountability, clarity and a hands-on understanding of the work, focused on building teams capable of delivering secure, reliable and scalable digital services that support long-term business growth and customer trust. He holds a degree in Computer Engineering and a master's in Analytics & Business Intelligence from the Politecnico di Milano, alongside a master's in Leadership, Empowerment & Change Management from 24ORE Business School. Outside of his executive role, Samuele maintains two open-source Home Assistant integrations on HACS and undertakes independent consulting engagements across cybersecurity, infrastructure, home automation and Home Assistant.
